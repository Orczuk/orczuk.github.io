Letters to Mom

Release Date: April, 2021
Status: Complete

You've written a sweet letter to your mom and just as you've put it in the mailbox it gets blown away by a passing car. Make your way back with the help of the breeze as you spread motherly love to all you meet across your journey.

Developed for College Game Jam, placing 8th out of the 85 entries.

inFamous NES Demake

Release Date: March, 2021
Status: Complete

inFamous NES Demake is inspired by the PS3 original game inFamous developed by Sucker Punch Productions. It's goal was to bringing the parkour action from PS3 all the way back to the NES. Allowing the player to jump, climb and slide across city rooftops while either saving or dooming the population of Empire City.

Developed by a three man team for Retro Jam 2021, placing 52 out of th 141 entries.

Adonis

Release Date: January, 2021
Status: Complete

Adonis is a shoot-em-up focused on collecting and ejecting add ons that provide great help at a great cost. You now know how much life you have left, but is it worth it being a larger target at your enemy's target? The decision is up to you.

Developed by a three man team for the Blackthornprod Game Jam #3, placing 444th out of th 1,071 entries.

Thousand Strike Revenge

Release Date: August, 2020
Status: Complete

Take part in Buddy’s journey towards self-improvement and decide whether he will use his new found power for good or evil. Live the work out montage that skyrockets Buddy’s abilities zero to hero and prove that you have what it takes to beat the Bully. Thousand Strike Revenge was created as part of the PB Game Jam 5.

Greedy Gobo

Release Date: July, 2020
Status: Complete

Gobo is in deep need of coins and the nearby Abbots seem to have their purses jingling with the precious trinkets. Use your phone’s gyroscopic controls to swish and swerve around Knights, Abbots and their holy symbols in attempt to grow Gobo’s horde of gold.

Live Sheets

Release Date: January, 2021
Status: Ongoing Development

Live Sheets is an image processing tool to help out in game development. Allowing you to test your sprite sheets in a quick and efficient way. Simply insert a sprite sheet and see the animations come to life.

Software

Unofficial Paladins Companion App

Release Date: June, 2020
Status: Ongoing Development

Bring your favorite Hero Shooter on the go! Spawn a random champion and learn their abilities and skills. Build or auto generate decks to experience surprising shifts in the game play and preload your arsenal before the game even starts. The Paladins Companion App is here to put your favorite Hero Shooter in your pocket. Unofficial Paladins Companion App fan app used to implement some features that would be great to see in game but also are great to have at your fingertips. All the visual assets belong to Evil Mojo Games and HiRez Studios.
